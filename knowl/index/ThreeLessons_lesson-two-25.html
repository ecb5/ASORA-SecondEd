Suppose that \(r_1\) and \(r_2\) are solutions of \(ax^2+bx+c=0\text{.}\) Without loss of generality suppose that \(a>0\text{.}\) Suppose further that \(r_1\ge r_2\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align*} ax^2+bx+c \amp = a(x-r_1)(x-r_2)\\ \amp = a\left[x^2-(r_1+r_2)x+(r_1+r_2)^2-(r_1-r_2)^2-3r_1r_2\right]. \end{align*}

\begin{align} r_1+r_2\amp = -\frac{b}{a}\tag{7}\\ \end{align}
and
\begin{align} r_1-r_2 \amp = \sqrt{\left(\frac{b}{a}\right)^2-\frac{4c}{a}}\text{.}\tag{8} \end{align}

\begin{align*} r_1\amp =\frac{-b+\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\\ r_2\amp =\frac{-b-\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\text{.} \end{align*}
