Theorem 11.3.9. Power Series Converge Uniformly.

Suppose \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n\) has radius of convergence \(r\) (where \(r\) could be \(\infty\) as well). Let \(b\) be any nonnegative real number with \(b\lt r\text{.}\) Then \(\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n\) converges uniformly on \([-b,b]\text{.}\)
