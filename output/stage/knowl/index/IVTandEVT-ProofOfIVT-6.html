Problem 9.2.3.

We can modify the proof of the case \(f(a)\leq v\leq f(b)\) into a proof of the IVT for the case \(f(a)\geq v\geq f(b)\text{.}\) However, there is a sneakier way to prove this case by applying the case we have proven to the function \(-f\text{.}\) Do this to prove the IVT for the case \(f(a)\geq v\geq f(b)\text{.}\)
