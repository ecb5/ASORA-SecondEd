Theorem 12.2.1. Weak Nested Interval Property.

Let \(\left(\left[a_n, b_n\right]\right)_{n=1}^\infty\) be a sequence of closed, nested intervals which satisfy the first three conditions of the NIP but not the fourth. Then there is at least one \(c\in\RR\) such that \(c\in\left[a_n, b_n\right]\) for all \(n\in\NN\text{.}\)
