Problem 1.3.5. Fermat’s Little Theorem, step 3.

Let \(p\) be a prime number and let \(k\) be an integer with \(1\le k\le p-1\text{.}\)
Use Problem 1.3.4 to prove that \(p\left|{p \choose{}k}\right.\text{,}\) where \({p \choose{}k}\) is the binomial coefficient \(\frac{p!}{k!(p-k)!}\text{.}\)
Hint.
We know \(p\,|p\,!\text{,}\) so \(p\,|{p \choose{}k}k!(p-k)!\text{.}\) How does the previous result apply?
